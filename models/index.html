01 — ABOUT MINDBRIDGE

What is MindBridge?

MindBridge is a browser-based, zero-server mental wellness intelligence platform designed to detect, quantify, and track psychological distress using multi-modal AI inference. Built on peer-reviewed research in affective computing, clinical psychology, and signal processing, it democratizes access to mental health screening tools previously available only in clinical settings.

Designed for individuals, wellness circles, college counselors, and researchers, MindBridge processes text and vocal inputs entirely on-device — no data ever leaves the browser. This ensures HIPAA-compatible privacy by architecture, not policy.

How MindBridge Processes Your Data

A robust 5-stage multi-modal inference pipeline converts raw user input into a clinically-informed composite risk score — entirely on-device.

Text input (free-form journal, Hindi/English) and/or vocal audio via WebAudio API MediaRecorder. Optional PHQ-9 / GAD-7 structured questionnaire responses.

Emotion AI Inference

DistilRoBERTa transformer model (Transformers.js, ONNX runtime) classifies text into 6 emotion categories: Sadness, Joy, Love, Anger, Fear, Surprise. Falls back to NLP-inferred profile if model loading.

Vocal Biomarker Analysis

Prosodic feature extraction from raw PCM audio: fundamental frequency (F0), jitter, shimmer, speech rate, energy envelope, and pause ratio. Correlated with dysphonia markers in clinical literature.

F0/JitterShimmerProsody

05
📊

Composite Score Fusion

Weighted fusion: Score = 0.4×EmotionAI + 0.4×NLP + 0.2×Vocal. Maps to 4-level clinical risk: Low / Moderate / High / Severe with actionable recommendations and helpline escalation.

About MindBridge

Web implementation & extension of a research paper submitted for conference review — Explainable AI for early mental health prediction in India · DTU, 2025

📄
Research Foundation — Submitted for Conference Review

Explainable AI For Early Prediction of Anxiety and Depression in India: A Dual-Modality Framework

Srimant Bhardwaj & Dr. Navneeta Bharadvaja · Dept. of Biotechnology, Delhi Technological University

The paper proposes a two-phase XAI pipeline: Phase 1 uses XGBoost + SMOTE on structured lifestyle & socio-demographic data (1,200 records, 79.95% accuracy). Phase 2 uses TF-IDF + XGBoost on 53,000+ unstructured text statements (93.73% accuracy, 96.10% precision). SHAP provides global feature importance; LIME provides instance-level word attribution. MindBridge is the browser-based implementation and extension of this work.

XGBoost + SMOTE TF-IDF NLP · 93.73% SHAP + LIME India-Specific Under Review · DTU 2025
⚠️ Scope boundary: The underlying research paper is submitted for conference review and not yet published. The trained XGBoost models run server-side on Python. This browser app uses research-inspired heuristics that conceptually approximate the paper's methodology — not the exact trained models. PHQ-9 & GAD-7 remain WHO-validated instruments.
🧬

NLP Text Analysis Paper Phase 2

Paper: TF-IDF vectorization on 53,000+ text statements → XGBoost classifier → 93.73% accuracy, 96.10% precision. MindBridge: Browser-side TF-IDF heuristic + 200+ word bilingual lexicon as an approximation. LIME word attribution implemented natively.

📊

Lifestyle Risk Analysis Paper Phase 1

Paper: XGBoost + SMOTE on 1,200 structured socio-demographic records (sleep, activity, stress, social support) → 79.95% accuracy. SHAP identified top risk features. MindBridge Tracker implements this as a live daily logging system.

🎙️

Vocal Biomarkers Paper Future Work

The paper explicitly listed vocal feature integration as future work. MindBridge implements this early: WebAudio API extracts pitch, RMS energy, jitter & shimmer in real-time. This is an experimental browser-side extension of the paper's stated direction.

🤖

BERT Emotion AI Extended

Not in the original paper. MindBridge adds a BERT-based deep learning layer (Xenova/bert-base-uncased-finetuned-emotion via Transformers.js) for multi-class emotion detection — a capability extension beyond the paper's TF-IDF binary classification approach.

📊 Paper → Web App: What Maps to What

Phase 1
Structured Lifestyle Risk Analysis — 79.95% accuracy

In paper: XGBoost + SMOTE on 1,200 records of sleep, physical activity, financial stress & social support. SHAP reveals top risk predictors globally.
In MindBridge: The Lifestyle Tracker implements this as a live daily logging system — same variables, user-driven input.

Phase 2
Behavioral Text Analysis (NLP) — 93.73% accuracy · 96.10% precision

In paper: TF-IDF vectorization on 53,000+ unstructured text statements → XGBoost classifier. LIME highlights word-level risk contributors.
In MindBridge: Browser TF-IDF heuristic + 200+ word bilingual lexicon approximates this. LIME-style word highlighting implemented natively in the Report view.

Future Work
Vocal Biomarker Integration — Implemented early in MindBridge

In paper: Explicitly listed as future work — "incorporating vocal features to better assess the emotions driven for anxiety and depression."
In MindBridge: Real-time WebAudio API extracts pitch, RMS energy, jitter & shimmer in-browser. Experimental early implementation of the paper's stated future direction.

Extended
New Additions Beyond the Paper's Scope

BERT emotion AI (deep learning emotion layer), PHQ-9 + GAD-7 WHO-validated screening, bilingual Hindi-English support, private journal, CBT breathing & grounding exercises, 30-day mood tracker, crisis helplines for India, and a fully offline PWA.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

MindBridge is an educational and supportive tool. The PHQ-9 and GAD-7 are WHO-validated clinical instruments — their scores carry scientific meaning. The composite AI risk score (combining Emotion AI + NLP + Vocal) is a research-inspired heuristic with custom-weighted parameters and has not been externally validated in a clinical study. It is indicative only and should never replace consultation with a licensed psychiatrist or mental health professional. If you are in crisis, please contact a helpline immediately.
